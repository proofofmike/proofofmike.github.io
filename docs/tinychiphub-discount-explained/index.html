TinyChipHub discount claim verification

This page exists solely to explain why some discount claims shown on third-party coupon sites do not apply when tested at TinyChipHub checkout. It is not the main TinyChipHub page on this site.

For the primary TinyChipHub page on this site — including miner notes and the verified discount information — go here:
https://proofofmike.com/tch/

Many coupon and affiliate sites reuse old codes, show “up to” percentages, or publish generic discount claims that do not reflect what is actually applied during checkout.

The only reliable way to confirm a discount is to enter a code at checkout and observe what is applied before payment.
