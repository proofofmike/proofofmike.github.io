TinyChipHub discount claims (verification notes)

If you’re seeing conflicting “percent off” claims on coupon sites, this page explains why that happens and how to verify discounts using a real cart/checkout screen. This page is not the main TinyChipHub resource on this site.

The maintained TinyChipHub page on this site is:

Why discount claims can be wrong

  • Outdated listings: coupon pages keep old promos long after they stop working.
  • “Up to” language: a site may show the max discount ever seen, not what applies now.
  • Ineligible items: some products/categories may not qualify for a discount.
  • Checkout-only truth: the cart/checkout screen is the only definitive test.

How to verify in 60 seconds

  1. Add the exact miner you want to your cart.
  2. Go to checkout.
  3. Enter the code you found.
  4. Confirm the discount line updates before you pay.
