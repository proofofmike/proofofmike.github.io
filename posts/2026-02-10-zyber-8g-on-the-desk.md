# Zyber 8G on the Desk

Powered up a **Zyber 8G Premium** and added it to my desk setup today.  
I wanted to jot down some early notes now that it’s online and settling in.

---

## ⚡ Early Numbers

On stock settings, it’s consistently showing **~10 TH/s**.  
Still very early, but everything looks stable so far.

This brings my desk setup to right around **26 TH/s total**, which is wild considering this used to be garage-only territory for me.

---

## 🎨 How It Looks

Anyone who’s followed my content for a while knows I’m partial to the NerdQaxe++.

The red PCB, orange fan, burgundy shroud — I’ve said it a hundred times, that thing just *pops*. It’s always the first miner people ask about when they see my desk.

The Zyber doesn’t lean into color the same way, but it has its own presence.  
It looks different. It’s not just a vertical PCB on a stand like a NerdQaxe or Bitaxe. The layout gives it personality, and it feels more like a finished piece of hardware than a dev board on display.

---

## 🧠 First Impressions

I’m genuinely excited about this one.

It’s the most hashrate I’ve had in a single miner in a number of years — and the first time that kind of hash has been sitting on my desk instead of tucked away in a garage.

I’ll share more once I’ve got real runtime and data behind it.
